Mindful Walking Session (20 Minutes)

Join us for a rejuvenating 20-minute mindful walking session where you'll learn to connect with your surroundings and cultivate mindfulness.

Session Breakdown

  • 1. Introduction (2 minutes)
  • 2. Warm-Up (3 minutes)
  • 3. Mindful Walking Meditation (10 minutes)
  • 4. Reflection and Sharing (3 minutes)
  • 5. Closing (2 minutes)

Mindfulness Prompts

During the walking meditation, focus on these prompts:

  • “Feel the ground beneath your feet with each step.”
  • “Notice the movement of your body as you walk.”
  • “Breathe in deeply, feeling the air fill your lungs.”
  • “Observe the sights and sounds around you without judgment.”
